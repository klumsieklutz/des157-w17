Weaving the Fabric of Hope

A Hopeful Encounter, Inc. and Revived Fashion Show has partnered up to host a dazzeling evening dedicated to enhancing the education of children. Revived will be hosting a fashion show inspired by the various Laotian ethnic groups and featuring a talented team of artists.

Put on your fancy shoes and join us in giving the gift of opportunity through education as we raise funds to build our second school in rural Laos. We will unveal our second school's location and blueprint on the evening of March 18, 2017.

Funds raised from this event will benefit the construction of the school, well, septic, and electricity connection. The total cost for this school will cost approximately $35,000. (This calculation is based on the current exchange rate in Laos.)

Help us build this school! Lets make early education a priority!

The First Encounter Gala will consist of:

  • Complimentary dinner
  • Assortment of ethnic h'oderves
  • Variety of raffle prizes
  • Multiple guest speakers
  • Talented local singers and musicians
  • Community dance groups
  • Fashion Show presented by Revived Fashion Show ...and more!
